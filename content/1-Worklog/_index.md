**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [AWS IAM, EC2 deployment, VPC networking, and Site-to-Site VPN fundamentals](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [High Availability, Scalability, RDS, Aurora, ElastiCache, Route 53, and Solutions Architecture](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Amazon S3 storage, CloudFront, Global Accelerator, Storage Gateway, FSx, and integration services (SQS, SNS, Step Functions)](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Containers on AWS (ECS, EKS), Serverless Architecture (Lambda, API Gateway), and Database services (RDS, DynamoDB, Redshift, ElastiCache)](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Data & Analytics (Athena, Glue, Kinesis), Machine Learning (SageMaker, Rekognition), Monitoring (CloudWatch, CloudTrail), and Advanced IAM](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [AWS Security & Encryption (KMS, CloudHSM, Shield, WAF), VPC Networking, and Disaster Recovery & Backup strategies](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Advanced Solutions Architecture, IoT Core, Ground Station, RoboMaker, and AWS Whitepapers](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Final AWS Cloud Journey summary report, full AWS system architecture design, and review of Week 1-8](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Project Proposal completion and AWS Architecture Diagram design](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Week 11 Worklog](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Week 12 Worklog](1.12-week12/)
